ABOUT

Roopesh Pathalil was born into a hardworking farming family in Kerala, as the second child of Ravindran, a dedicated farmer, and Shyamala, a homemaker. Growing up in a modest, middle-class family, Roopesh learned early on the values of hard work, responsibility, and resilience.

He pursued his primary education in a government school, followed by Plus Two in Science, before completing a Diploma in E & C Engineering from Nityananda Polytechnic, Kanhangad. However, his life was marked by personal hardships, the most significant being his mother’s battle with liver cirrhosis. Her prolonged illness and eventual passing deeply impacted Roopesh, shaping his outlook on life and his future ambitions.

A Life-Changing Incident

In his early twenties, Roopesh faced one of the toughest times of his life. His mother, Shyamala, the family's pillar, was suffering from liver cirrhosis. Her illness weighed heavily on their household, and every day was a struggle to ensure her comfort. One night, as Roopesh cared for her, the power suddenly went out. Given Shyamala's fragile health, this was more than an inconvenience—it sparked deep anxiety. The darkness that enveloped their home mirrored Roopesh's fear and helplessness as he tried to determine the cause of the power failure. He contacted every electrician he could think of, hoping that someone would come to their aid. But it was late, and no one was available to help. The night stretched on, filled with worry and sleeplessness. His mother’s condition added to the gravity of the situation, making the hours seem even longer. The next day, an electrician arrived and quickly identified a tripped RCCB as the issue. He bypassed it to restore power, compromising the home's safety with a temporary fix. Days later, a family member received an electric shock, highlighting the risks of neglecting safety for convenience.This incident deeply affected Roopesh. He couldn't forget that night—the fear, helplessness, and realization of their jeopardized safety. He vowed that no one should endure what his family experienced, facing darkness and danger alone in critical situations.
This experience planted the seed for what would eventually become the Rapid Action Team (RAT). Roopesh envisioned a service that would be there for people in their most vulnerable moments, offering not just technical solutions but also the reassurance of safety and care. RAT would be more than just a business; it would be a social commitment to protect and serve the community.

STATUS

Building a Technical Career

After his mother’s passing, Roopesh focused on building his technical skills. He joined Amrita College of Engineering in 2013 as a driver and electrician, progressing to supervisor and electrical engineer roles. He later worked as a solar installation engineer, expanding his expertise. Br. Muralidharan, Head of the Electrical Department, became his mentor, teaching him electrical engineering and the value of helping others.

Spiritual Awakening

Roopesh’s life was shaped by both technical growth and a profound spiritual journey. He spent a year studying under Adimagi maha chandala Baba Malavari Muthan, learning the Tantrik Malavaram and Vaidik systems. Initiated as Param Sri Maha Chandala Dasananda Adimargi, he committed to serving society and spirituality. This experience clarified his vision of life and reaffirmed his desire to contribute positively to the world.

The Birth of Rapid Action Team (RAT)

Driven by his personal experiences, Roopesh founded Rapid Action Team (RAT) in Kochi, a 24-hour maintenance service providing prompt electrical support to homes and businesses. Inspired by his family's past struggles, he aimed to ensure that others would not endure similar hardships. RAT prioritized safety, reliability, and quick response, transforming into a mission to serve the community and safeguard its members.

Expanding His Vision

Building on the success of RAT, Roopesh expanded his entrepreneurial efforts by founding Aigerpeak LLP, a company offering premium electrical and plumbing services, particularly for larger-scale projects. Aigerpeak LLP provides the same high standards of service that RAT was built on, ensuring quality and dependability in all its projects.

Culinary Ventures

Roopesh’s entrepreneurial spirit wasn’t limited to the electrical field. He also ventured into the culinary space by partnering to create Chef Sandhya’s Kitchen, a cloud kitchen that serves delicious and healthy local food. This venture allowed Roopesh to diversify his portfolio while staying true to his core values of quality and service.

A Life Dedicated to Service

Roopesh Pathalil leads several successful businesses grounded in trust, quality, and service. His passion for accessible and safe electrical services drives his work at Rapid Action Team, Aigerpeak LLP, and Chef Sandhya’s Kitchen, improving clients' lives. From a farming family in Kerala to a successful entrepreneur and social leader, Roopesh’s story exemplifies perseverance, resilience, and community dedication.
