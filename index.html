ABOUT

Roopesh Pathalil was born into a hardworking farming family in Kerala, the second child of Ravindran, a farmer, and Shyamala, a homemaker. Raised in a modest middle-class home, he learned the values of hard work, responsibility, and resilience early on.

He completed his primary education at a government school and pursued Plus Two in Science before earning a Diploma in E&C Engineering from Nityananda Polytechnic, Kanhangad. His life was profoundly shaped by personal hardships, especially his mother's battle with liver cirrhosis, which had a lasting impact on his outlook and ambitions.

A Life-Changing Incident

In his early twenties, Roopesh faced a difficult time as his mother, Shyamala, battled liver cirrhosis. One night, while caring for her, a power outage caused deep anxiety. With no electricians available, the darkness heightened his fear and worry as he struggled to fix the issue, compounded by his mother’s fragile health.
After a tripped RCCB caused a power outage, an electrician restored power by bypassing it, compromising safety. Days later, a family member received an electric shock, underscoring the dangers of prioritizing convenience over safety. This incident deeply impacted Roopesh, who vowed that no one should face such risks alone. This experience inspired the creation of the Rapid Action Team (RAT), a service dedicated to supporting people during vulnerable moments. Roopesh envisioned RAT as more than a business; it would be a commitment to ensuring community safety and care.

STATUS

Building a Technical Career

After his mother’s passing, Roopesh honed his technical skills. He joined Amrita College of Engineering in 2013 as a driver and electrician, later becoming a supervisor and electrical engineer. With guidance from Br. Muralidharan, he gained expertise in solar installations and learned the value of helping others.

Spiritual Awakening

Roopesh’s life combined technical growth with a deep spiritual journey. He studied for a year under Baba Malavari Muthan, learning the Tantrik and Vaidik systems. Initiated as Param Sri Maha Chandala Dasananda Adimargi, this experience clarified his life vision and strengthened his commitment to serving society.

The Birth of Rapid Action Team (RAT)

Roopesh founded Rapid Action Team (RAT) in Kochi, offering 24-hour electrical support to homes and businesses. Inspired by his family's struggles, RAT focused on safety, reliability, and quick response, becoming a mission to serve and protect the community.

Expanding His Vision

After the success of RAT, Roopesh started Aigerpeak LLP, offering electrical and plumbing services for bigger projects. Aigerpeak follows the same high standards as RAT, ensuring quality and reliability.

Culinary Ventures

Roopesh’s entrepreneurial spirit wasn’t limited to the electrical field. He also ventured into the culinary space by partnering to create Chef Sandhya’s Kitchen, a cloud kitchen that serves delicious and healthy local food. This venture allowed Roopesh to diversify his portfolio while staying true to his core values of quality and service.

A Life Dedicated to Service

Roopesh Pathalil runs successful businesses focused on trust, quality, and service. His dedication to safe electrical services guides his work at Rapid Action Team, Aigerpeak LLP, and Chef Sandhya’s Kitchen, enhancing clients' lives. From his farming background in Kerala to becoming an entrepreneur and community leader, Roopesh’s journey showcases perseverance and resilience.
